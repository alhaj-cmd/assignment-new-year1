About Us

Sirajganj District of Rajshahi Division in Bangladesh. Sirajganj is well connected with Dhaka and other major cities of the country through road and railway transport. There are plenty of government and private organizations buses available at Sirajganj Town Bus Stand to reach nearby regions. Jamuna Bridge East Railway Station is important railway station among the surrounding regions of Sirajganj situated about 30 kms away from Sirajganj town centre. The distance between Sirajganj and its nearest airport ‘Shahjalal International Airport’, Dhaka is about 128 km. Taxis, Autos and Jeeps are easily accessible at Sirajganj town to reach airport and railway station at reasonable rent.
